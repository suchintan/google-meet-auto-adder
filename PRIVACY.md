# Privacy Policy — Auto Google Meet Adder

**Last updated:** March 4, 2026

## Data Collection

Auto Google Meet Adder does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data, browsing history, or usage information.

## What the Extension Does

This extension runs a content script on `calendar.google.com` that detects when you are creating or editing a Google Calendar event and automatically adds a Google Meet video conferencing link. It interacts only with the page DOM in your browser.

## Storage

The only data stored is your enable/disable preference, saved locally via Chrome's built-in sync storage API. This preference contains no personal information.

## Network Requests

This extension makes **zero** network requests. It does not communicate with any external servers, APIs, or analytics services.

## Third Parties

No data is shared with third parties.

## Changes

If this policy changes, the update will be posted here.
